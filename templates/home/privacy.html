üîê Our Privacy Commitment

EmotionSpace is built on the foundation of trust and anonymity. We understand that sharing personal thoughts and emotions requires a safe space where your privacy is absolutely protected. This policy explains how we safeguard your information while providing a supportive community platform.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account with EmotionSpace, we collect:

  • Email address (for account verification and security)
  • Username (chosen by you, can be pseudonymous)
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Account creation date and time

Content You Share

We collect the content you voluntarily share on our platform:

  • Confessions, posts, and journal entries
  • Comments and interactions with other users' content
  • Reactions (likes, support reactions)
  • Messages sent through our support systems

Technical Information

To ensure platform security and functionality, we automatically collect:

  • IP address (immediately anonymized for privacy)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, features used)
  • Log files for security and error monitoring

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve our mental health support services:

Service Provision

  • Maintaining and operating the EmotionSpace platform
  • Enabling you to create, share, and interact with content
  • Providing personalized recommendations for supportive content
  • Facilitating connections with mental health resources

Safety & Security

  • Protecting against harassment, abuse, or harmful content
  • Preventing fraud and maintaining account security
  • Monitoring for violations of community guidelines
  • Responding to legal requests when legally required

Platform Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Developing new features that support mental wellness
  • Conducting research to better serve our community (always anonymized)

3. Anonymity & Protection

üé≠ Anonymous by Design

Your confessions and personal content are never directly linked to your real identity. We use advanced anonymization techniques to ensure your emotional expressions remain private.

How We Protect Your Anonymity

  • Pseudonymous Display: Content is shown with anonymous usernames or "Anonymous" labels
  • Data Separation: Personal account information is stored separately from content data
  • IP Masking: IP addresses are immediately anonymized upon collection
  • Content Isolation: We use technical measures to prevent linking content back to specific users
  • Staff Access Controls: Limited employees have access to personal data, and only when necessary

When Anonymity May Be Limited

We may need to identify users only in extreme circumstances:

  • Immediate risk of self-harm or harm to others
  • Legal obligations (court orders, law enforcement requests)
  • Violations of community guidelines that threaten other users

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party providers who help us operate EmotionSpace:

  • Cloud hosting services (with strict data protection agreements)
  • Email service providers (for account notifications only)
  • Analytics providers (with anonymized data only)
  • Security services (for fraud prevention and platform safety)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when legally required:

  • In response to valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • To prevent imminent harm to individuals or public safety
  • To protect our legal rights and comply with applicable laws

Emergency Situations

If we believe someone is in immediate danger, we may contact:

  • Emergency services
  • Mental health crisis intervention teams
  • Designated emergency contacts (if provided)

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Servers: Data is stored on secure, regularly monitored servers
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access for all staff
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Backup: Secure, encrypted backups with limited access

Organizational Safeguards

  • Staff training on privacy and security best practices
  • Confidentiality agreements for all personnel
  • Incident response procedures for potential data breaches
  • Regular security policy reviews and updates

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Account Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a common format

Privacy Controls

  • Choose your level of anonymity for posts
  • Control notification preferences
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Manage cookie preferences

Content Management

  • Edit or delete your posts and comments
  • Control who can interact with your content
  • Report inappropriate content or behavior

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function:

  • User authentication and session management
  • Security features and fraud prevention
  • Basic site functionality and navigation

Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how users interact with our platform:

  • Anonymized usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Feature effectiveness analysis

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings or our privacy preferences center. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit platform functionality.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

Active Accounts

  • Account information: Retained while your account is active
  • Content: Stored according to your privacy settings
  • Technical logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days

Deleted Accounts

  • Personal information: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Content: Removed or anonymized immediately
  • Backup data: Purged within 90 days

Legal Requirements

Some information may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (fraud prevention, safety).

9. Children's Privacy

üë∂ Special Protection for Minors

EmotionSpace is designed for users 13 years and older. We take extra precautions to protect younger users' privacy and safety.

Age Requirements

  • Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account
  • Users under 18 are considered minors and receive additional privacy protections
  • Parental consent may be required in certain jurisdictions

Additional Protections

  • Enhanced content moderation for age-appropriate material
  • Limited data collection for users under 18
  • Special procedures for handling safety concerns involving minors

10. International Users

EmotionSpace serves users globally and complies with international privacy laws:

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

  • Legal basis for processing personal data
  • Enhanced rights for EU residents
  • Data Protection Officer contact information available
  • Right to lodge complaints with supervisory authorities

CCPA Compliance (California Residents)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Data Transfers

When we transfer data internationally, we use appropriate safeguards including standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions.

11. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements:

Notification Process

  • Email notification for significant changes
  • Prominent notice on our platform
  • 30-day advance notice for material changes
  • Opportunity to review and accept changes

Version Control

Previous versions of our privacy policy are archived and available upon request. The "Last Updated" date at the bottom indicates when changes were made.
